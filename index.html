Yahan se, yahan tak

  1. Wo Din

    Jis din tumne mujhe qubool kiya, us din se main sirf tumhara ho gaya.

  2. 18 June

    Jab humari baat cheet shuru hui — aur ek naye safar ki shuruaat hui.

  3. Aahista Aahista

    Hum dono ek dusre ke kareeb aate gaye, chahe kitni bhi galatfehmiyan kyun na hui, kitni bhi ladai jhagde hue.

  4. Mushkil Waqt

    Shaadi mein bhi bohot si mushkilein aayi, lekin humari mohabbat ne kabhi haar nahi maani.

  5. Alhamdulillah

    Sab kuch theek ho gaya — aur sabse khaas baat, hamari marzi ke mutabiq hua.

  6. Aaj Tak

    Us din se leke ab tak, tum mere sath ho — aur main tumhare bagair adhoora hoon.
